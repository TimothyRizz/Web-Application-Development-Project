Product Details

Key Features
  • Processor: Equipped with a 1.4GHz quad-core ARM Cortex-A53 CPU, providing a substantial performance boost.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Integrated dual-band wireless LAN (2.4GHz and 5GHz) and Bluetooth 4.2 for seamless wireless communication.
  • Fast Ethernet: Gigabit Ethernet port for high-speed wired networking.
  • Memory: 1GB LPDDR2 SDRAM to support multitasking and memory-intensive applications.
  • Expansion: 40 GPIO pins for connecting various sensors, displays, and accessories.
  • USB Ports: Four USB 2.0 ports for connecting peripherals such as a keyboard, mouse, and external storage.
  • MicroSD Slot: A microSD card slot for the operating system and data storage.
  • HDMI Output: Full-sized HDMI port for connecting to a monitor or TV.
  • Power: Powered via micro-USB or PoE (Power over Ethernet) with the addition of a PoE HAT.
